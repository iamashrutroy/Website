Historical Highlights

The Australia national cricket team played its first ODI match against England on January 5, 1975, at the Melbourne Cricket Ground (MCG). Though Australia lost in the final of the 1975 World Cup, they claimed their first World Cup title in 1987.

  • Most Wickets: Glenn McGrath - 384 wickets
